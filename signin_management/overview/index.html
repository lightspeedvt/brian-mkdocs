Overview

The following sections will demonstrate how to edit your sign in pages. At this time a LightSpeed VT employee will create your original

image - select site

Basic Info

image - basic info - full

  1. Sub Domain

    • This is the domain where your users will login. This field is locked, if you would like to update it, please contact LSVT Client Care.

  2. Account

    • Your account is a home for your collection of Systems, and within those Systems your different themes. It is basically the Parent of the bunch. This tells us if your username is located within a system residing in this account, you will have access to this sign in page.

  3. Email Template Theme

    • With this option, you can choose to change the theme of the "Retreive Password" email. If you have Multiple Systems with Multiple themes and sign in pages, this maybe be useful for you. Otherwise it will usually just be one default theme.

  4. Name

    • This is the name that will appear on the Title of the site (in the browser) and also in the admin area so you can easily identify it

  5. Show Terms of Service

    • This is optional if you'd like us to turn on a generic terms of service link. (typically the user has already agreed to the terms of service before they arrive at this point, so we included the option to hide it.)

  6. Show Privacy Policy

    • This is similar to #5, it is an optional toggle.

  7. Show Social Media Sign In Integrations

    • If you would like your users to be able to use Google, Facebook, Twitter, or LinkedIn credentials to sign in to LightSpeed, you should enable this option. As long as the email address on their social account matches the one entered in LightSpeed, they will be able to login with it.

  8. Active

    • This is just an easy toggle to turn the page on or off

  9. Details

    • This will allow you to see who created the site and who was the last to update it.

Content

Header

  1. Logo
    • Typically this will be the same logo we use when building your System.
  2. Front Door Link
    • This is usually your front door / main marketing site for your System.

Page

  1. Background
    • Choose a background image, typically we will match this image to the one we use inside of your System.
  2. Join Link
    • If you have a direct link to a "Join" or "Sign Up" page for your system you can enter that here.
  3. Join Link Target
    • If you want to open the above link in a new window, or use the same window (we usually suggest a new window).
  4. Join Link Name
    • The default Join Link name is "Join Now" - you can use this field to override that if you want to customize it.

Home

  1. Sign in Text
    • This text appears above the sign in area, you can customize it to how you see fit, a good default for this is something like, "Please enter your username and password below to sign in."

Help

  1. Support Message
    • Use this text to enter important information about your support hours or other information.
  2. Support Page.
    • Here you can enter a link to your support site (or leave blank if you linked it in the above text)
  3. Email
    • Enter your Support email address here.
  4. Phone
    • Enter your Support phone number here.
