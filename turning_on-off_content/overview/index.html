Overview

GOOD NEWS!

There are Admin tools to do bulk content activations and deactivations for lots Locations at a time!

BAD NEWS!

We’re not quite ready to have them LIVE yet for Super User B’s (sorry).

BETTER NEWS!

The LSVT Client Care Dept. can assist you in bulk content changes - simply give us a call or an email, and we’ll be happy to assist!

We’ll just ask you what the list of Course ID’s you want activated (or deactivated) and a list of Location ID’s or just tell us - “All Locations.”
